Fons Vitae 2 Senior High School, also known as Marsudirini North Jakarta, was established on June 28, 1971, located at Jl. Kramat Jaya No. 1B, Koja, North Jakarta, in the same complex as Marsudirini Tanjung Priok School.

Vision and Mission

Vision: Fons Vitae 2 Senior High School aims to become an Excellent, Disciplined, Intellectual, Faithful, Humanistic, and Nature-Loving School.


Mission:


  • To develop intellectual intelligence by equipping students with knowledge and information technology skills.

  • To develop spiritual intelligence through the integration of learning with Christian faith and Marsudirinian values.

  • To develop emotional intelligence by fostering sensitivity, self-control, strong discipline, independence, and responsibility.

School Activities

Our extracurricular activities include:


  • Scout Movement

  • Student Council (OSIS)

  • Badminton Club

  • Basketball Club

  • Choir
